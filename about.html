What is Meetwoyou?

Meetwoyou is a modern social network that brings together the things people actually use every day: a personal feed, short videos (Reels), stories, private messages with voice notes, and an advanced analytics dashboard so creators understand who is engaging with their work.

Built with

  • Pure HTML, CSS and JavaScript — no build step required.
  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore for realtime data.
  • Cloudinary for media storage.
  • Service workers for offline-first performance.
