Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Meetwoyou ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy describes what we collect, why, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data — name, email, password (hashed by Firebase), photo, bio, optional birthday, gender, profession.
  • Content — posts, stories, comments, reels, messages, voice notes, reactions you create.
  • Usage data — likes, follows, video views, time on app, device type.
  • Approximate location — country and city derived from IP, used only for post insights (never precise GPS).

2. How we use it

  • Provide the service: feed, reels, messages, notifications, search.
  • Show creators aggregated insights about their own posts (viewer country/city, age range, gender split).
  • Detect abuse, spam, fraud and enforce our Terms.
  • Improve product performance and reliability.

3. Sharing

We do not sell your data. Limited sharing only with:

  • Firebase / Google Cloud — auth, database, hosting.
  • Cloudinary — media storage and delivery.
  • ipapi.co — approximate location lookup from IP.
  • Legal authorities when required by law.

4. Your controls

  • Edit or delete profile details, posts, stories and messages at any time.
  • Switch to a private account, hide active status, restrict who can DM you (Settings → Privacy).
  • Block users you don't want to interact with.
  • Download a JSON copy of your data (Settings → Data).
  • Permanently delete your account (Settings → Danger Zone).

5. Children

Meetwoyou is not intended for children under 13. Accounts found to belong to under-13 users will be removed.

6. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest on Firebase. No system is 100% secure; please use a strong password and enable email recovery.

7. Cookies & local storage

We use local storage and service workers to keep you signed in, cache the interface, and remember preferences such as theme. No third-party advertising cookies.

8. Changes

Material changes will be announced inside the app. Continued use after the update means you accept the new policy.
